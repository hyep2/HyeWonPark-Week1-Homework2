About Me



Welcome to my page. My name is Hye Won Park, and I am an aspiring software engineer. Here is a short bio about myself. I am a first-generation Korean American, who lived in Southern California for most of her life. I attended elementary, highschool, and college here. I was originally a pre-med student, leading me to get an undergraduate B.S. degree in Physiology at UCLA. During my gap year, I realized that medicine wasn't for me. Having had some background with coding at Troy High School for three years, where I learned Java, I wanted to try out coding again, as I have always enjoyed it. I decided to enroll in the UCI Coding Bootcamp this year 2022 so that I can jumpstart my career as a software engineer/web developer. I hope to learn the necessary skills and make many projects through this program to grow out my portfolio. Join me in this journey!
